Simpatico: A Text Simplification System for Senate and House Bills

Collantes, Hipe, Sorilla, Tolentino

Simpatico is a text simplification system that makes us of lexical and syntactic simplification methods in order to simplify legalese to plain English in which a majority of the Philippine population can understand. It makes use of various existing NLP tools in order to carry out tasks like multiword extraction and word sense disambiguation.
